The Team Behind Mentra — 04

Mentra was born inside ALMA — built for every team that trains.

Mentra AI was founded by Axel and Terah Bromley — the team behind ALMA LLC, a virtual-assistant company connecting talented Filipino professionals with North American businesses. Running ALMA, they lived the training problem firsthand: great teams are built on great training. Mentra is that conviction, turned into a product every team can use.

Axel
President & Founder, ALMA LLC

President & Founder of ALMA LLC, Axel brings 25+ years leading international business across four continents. He co-founded Mentra AI with his wife Terah to bring AI-powered microtraining to enterprise teams — and lectures on Global Business Strategy at BGSU.

Terah
Co-Founder & CEO, Mentra AI

Co-Founder & CEO of Mentra AI and a learning architect with 20+ years designing training that sticks. A former professor and corporate curriculum designer, Terah blends academic rigor with real-world execution to build workforce training for modern teams.

William Heath
Chief AI Officer, ALMA

As Chief AI Officer, William Heath brings 25+ years of software engineering across defense, aerospace, and robotics. A university-level educator and certification instructor, he builds with cutting-edge AI tools daily — shaping how intelligence runs through every layer of Mentra.
