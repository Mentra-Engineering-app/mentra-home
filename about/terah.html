Co-Founder & CEO, Mentra AI  ·  Co-Founder, HomeShare  ·  ALMA LLC

Terah Bromley is a seasoned business strategist and learning architect with over two decades of international experience spanning the United States, Asia, the Middle East, Eastern Europe, and Latin America. She holds a Master of Communication Studies from Arizona State University and a B.A. from Brigham Young University, and has delivered training programs across Croatia, the UAE, Nepal, Qatar, and the Philippines.

As Co-Founder and CEO of Mentra AI, Terah is building the future of workforce training — an AI-powered microlearning platform purpose-built for modern enterprise teams. Her background includes roles as a professor, corporate curriculum designer, national sales director, and franchise business coach, giving her a rare blend of academic rigor and real-world commercial execution.

Terah is also the founder of HomeShare, an innovative room-by-room rental platform addressing Canada's housing crisis in Alberta, and co-leads ALMA LLC alongside her husband, connecting Filipino virtual assistants with businesses across North America. She is passionate about unlocking human potential through intelligent systems, great training, and purposeful leadership.
