J. Axel Bromley
President & Founder, ALMA LLC  ·  Co-Founder, Mentra AI  ·  Co-Founder, HomeShare

J. Axel Bromley is an international business executive and entrepreneur with over 25 years of experience across Asia, Eastern Europe, the Middle East, South and Central America, and the Indian Subcontinent. His career spans more than two decades in the insurance industry, including 12+ years as a General Manager and Country Manager operating under geopolitical uncertainty, leading change management, corporate culture transformation, and start-up operations at scale.

Axel is the President and Founder of ALMA LLC (American Labor Management Association), a Virtual Assistant company built on a mission-driven model — connecting talented Filipino professionals with North American businesses while investing in their education, healthcare, and future. He co-founded Mentra AI alongside his wife Terah, bringing AI-powered microtraining to enterprise teams and insurance organizations worldwide.

An active builder and strategist, Axel also co-founded HomeShare, an innovative rental platform unlocking dormant housing inventory in Alberta, Canada. He is a seminary teacher and a devoted father who also serves as a university lecturer — teaching undergraduate Global Business Strategy and MBA-level Global Business Management at BGSU — bringing the same values of integrity, service, and long-term thinking to everything he builds.
